Neurology Department

Neurology is a branch of medicine concerned with the study and treatment of disorders of nervous system. The nervous system is a complex and sophisticated system that regulates and coordinates body activities. It has two major divisions:

• Central nervous system: the brain and spinal cord

• Peripheral nervous system: all other neural elements such as eyes, ears, skin, and other "sensory receptors"

 

A doctor who specializes in neurology is called a Neurologist. The neurologist treats the disorders that affect the brain, spinal cord, and nerves, such as-

o Cerebrovascular disease - stroke, etc.

o Demyelinating diseases of the central nervous system- multiple sclerosis, etc.

o Headache disorders

o Infections of the brain and peripheral nervous system

o Movement disorders - Parkinson's disease, tremors, chorea, imbalance, etc.

o Neurodegenerative disorders - Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, and Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (Lou Gehrig's disease))

o Seizure disorders- epilepsy

o Spinal cord disorders

o Speech and language disorders

o Memorydisorders

o Nerve disorders- neuropathies, etc.

o Musculardisorders- dystrophies, etc.

o Pain disorders- fibromyalgia, trigeminal neuralgia, radiculopathies

o Giddiness causes such as vertigo

o Brain tumours.
