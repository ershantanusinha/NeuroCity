Outdoor Checkup

The Neurocity Hospitalhas a full-fledged OPD department which has advanced facilities for providing various types of OPD services for our patients. With a dedicated team of specialists who handle various OPD services within our hospital.

Operation Theatre

The Operation Theatre is fitted with best in class equipments, innovative surgical microscope for highly demanding neurosurgical applications, operating lights, anesthesia work stations & advanced information management systems.

Pharmacy

We Provide 24Hr Pharmacy Service. The pharmacy department maintains an inventory of critical and life-saving drugs that are distributed and sold over the counter following all the rules and regulations as per the guidelines and norms of administration.

Laboratory

The Hospital has well-equipped labs for the entire gamut of diagnostic services in Hematology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, and Serology. It has 24 hrs laboratory with automated analyzers to perform all Biochemistry, ELISA, Pathology, Microbiology investigations.

Doctors

The team of doctors at Hospital is amongst the best in their respective areas of specialty. The highly qualified medical team offers comprehensive health care service using the latest equipments and techniques available in the region.

Emergency Care

The Hospital has 24 hrs of ambulance services fully equipped with Advanced Life Support System for all kinds of emergencies
