About Us

Welcome To The Neuro City Hospital

The Neurocity hospital is a NABH Entry Level Pre Accredited Hospital, 50-bedded multi-disciplinary & super speciality hospital With national & international repute. The Neurocity Hospital is famous for its ambiance, quality of care and high-level patient satisfaction. The state-of-the-art facilities coupled with latest equipments and renowned consultants ensures that patients from Varanasi,Purvanchal, various states of India and foreign countries come for highly specialized tertiary level treatment.

The Neurocity Hospital – A unit of Madison Neurocity Pvt. Ltd. has been established in 2014, under a team of Neurosurgeons, Neurologists, Neurointerventional surgeons, paramedics, and world-class equipment and facilities. Since its inception, many more specialties have been added and presently it is a multi-specialty Hospital.
